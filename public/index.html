Lissajous Curve Table

Named after Jules Antoine Lissajous, also known as Bowditch curve is the graph of a system of parametric equations which describe complex harmonic motion

using following formula:

𝑥 = r * sin(aθ + δ)
𝑦 = r * sin(bθ)

  • r is a radius of the circles
  • a and b are the rotation magnitudes (horizontal and vertical circles)
  • θ is the current angle of rotation
  • δ is π / 2
